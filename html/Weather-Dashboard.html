Weather Dashboard

Back to the main page.

This application was made to show understanding of and ability to recieve data from third party APIs. This application uses the Open Weather API to bring you the current weather of any city you look up.

Type in the city that you want to know the weather information of and it will respond with that cities current weather. Future plans are adding local storage, current city information as well as UV index.
